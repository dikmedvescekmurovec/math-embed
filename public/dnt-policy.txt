Math Embed does not track its customers over time and across third party websites to provide targeted advertising and
therefore does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals. Third parties that have content embedded on Math Embed’s
websites such as a social feature or a stock ticker may set cookies on a user’s browser and/or obtain information
about the fact that a web browser visited a specific Math Embed website from a certain IP address. Third parties
cannot collect any other personally identifiable information from Math Embed’s websites unless you provide it to them 
directly.
